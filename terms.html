The Matching System

Figure matches are generated by meow-me's acoustic analysis system, which scores vocalisations across five axes and matches the result against a curated library of 128 historical figures.

  • Results vary based on the recording. The same cat may receive different matches across sessions.
  • Historical figures are used in a fictional, humorous context. They are not affiliated with or endorsed by meow-me.
  • Descriptions of historical figures are original creative works. Any resemblance to official biographical material is incidental.
